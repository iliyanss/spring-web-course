Welcome to Buildershop

Building Shop is a family run business with experienced staff offering over 40 years experience in supplying building materials to the trade and public. The management having previously owned a Timber and Builders Merchants realised there was a niche in the market for a building material supplier who could respond faster to a builders needs as we appreciate it is not always possible to order goods well in advance and you may not have the time to fully capitalise on every order. We use a network of reliable suppliers & merchants to source any building material at a competitive price and deliver as soon as close to your preferred date as possible. We also offer bulk discounts for large orders. If you are unable to find what you are looking for we will do our best to find it for you.
